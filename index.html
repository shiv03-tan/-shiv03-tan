01 · About

Bridging AI with Clinical Medicine

I'm a PhD researcher at National Yunlin University of Science and Technology (YunTech), Taiwan, specializing in AI-driven biomedical imaging for non-invasive diagnostics. My current research focuses on predicting blood glucose levels using ultrasound, OCTA, MRI, and CT — without a single needle.

My work is grounded in real clinical collaboration — partnering with Hualien Tzu Chi Hospital to collect and validate data that bridges the gap between machine learning research and patient care.

I hold a Master's in Computer Science from YunTech and a Bachelor of Technology from BIT Mesra, India, backed by four scholarships — including the YunTech International Doctoral Scholarship (Outstanding Student), MOE Taiwan (Master's), and TATA Steel India (Bachelor's). I've also been recognized with the Best Paper Runner-Up at IEEE SmartIoT 2024 in Shenzhen, China.
